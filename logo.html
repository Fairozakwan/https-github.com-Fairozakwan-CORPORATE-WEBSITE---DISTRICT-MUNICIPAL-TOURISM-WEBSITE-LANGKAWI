The Functions of MPLBP

  • Planning and controlling development based on the Langkawi District Local Plan.
  • Licensing and controlling businesses, advertisements, and hawkers.
  • Coordinating and assisting development in Langkawi.
  • Controlling buildings, advertising, and signboards.
  • Supervising and maintaining the beautification of the city and landscape.
  • Supervising and maintaining public facilities such as markets, stalls, public toilets, children's playgrounds, bus stops, and recreational facilities.
  • Providing health services, food control, hygiene of food premises, or control of infectious diseases.
  • Improving basic facilities, upgrading villages, and encouraging viable economic activities.
  • Increasing assessment tax collection.
  • Monitoring solid waste disposal and city cleaning.
